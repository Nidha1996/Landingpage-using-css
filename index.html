Welcome To The Beach

Leave footprints of love and kindness wherever you go.

Beach offers so much more than pristine ocean waters.Embody blissful, barefoot, carefree days and glamorous star-filled nights. Dive in and join us for pure sand-surf-salty hedonism.Beach is family-friendly – enjoy fun-filled days of watersports, beachcombing, ocean spa treatments and private cabanas with views over the Indian ocean.Come sunset and immerse in sensual evenings enhanced by DJs, live music, cocktails and seafood feasts. When it comes to chilled-out luxe Beach sets a new high-vibe standard. Come join us…

A boutique beach on the shores of Gili Meno island at Reef. Visit our exclusive island hotspot and children chase crabs in the sand; indulge in a facial to the soothing soundtrack of the Indian Ocean; then slip on a sarong and head for dinner,where soulful jazz is served on the side.
A crescent of white sand leads to a tranquil blue lagoon, both exclusively available for the enjoyment of members. Curate your days with a personalised itinerary of experiences – Stand Up Paddle Yoga, sea kayaking and snorkeling. For children, our Three Monkeys Kids Club provides exciting, engaging activities for all ages.
